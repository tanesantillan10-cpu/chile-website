Modern Santiago

From the historic streets of Barrio Yungay to the soaring heights of the Andes skyline, Santiago is a city defined by its constant contrast and artistic evolution.

Barrio Lastarria

While Valparaíso is the "capital" of street art, Santiago’s neighborhoods like Barrio Lastarria offer a modern urban vibe with massive murals, boutique cafes, and historic architecture. It is a living museum where heritage meets contemporary street culture, creating an open-air gallery for all to explore.

Rising above these creative districts is the Sky Costanera, the tallest building in South America. It provides a sharp modern contrast to the colorful murals below, offering a 360-degree view that connects the urban sprawl to the majesty of the mountains.
