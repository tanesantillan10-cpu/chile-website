Central Valleys

TERMAS DE JAHUEL

Surrounded by vineyards and mountain scenery, Termas de Jahuel is one of Chile’s oldest and most renowned thermal resorts, celebrated since the 19th century for its mineral-rich waters.

The hot springs are complemented by spa treatments, wine therapy, and countryside tranquility, making it a perfect blend of relaxation and cultural immersion. Its proximity to Santiago allows visitors to easily escape the city and rejuvenate in a setting that combines wellness, history, and the charm of Chile’s central valleys.
