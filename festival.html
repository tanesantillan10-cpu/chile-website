LA TIRANA FESTIVAL

Location: Atacama Desert

La Tirana Festival is one of Chile’s most spectacular religious celebrations, held every July. Dedicated to the Virgen del Carmen, the festival draws tens of thousands of pilgrims who gather to witness vibrant processions, traditional dances, and elaborate costumes in a unique fusion of Catholic and Andean traditions.

FIESTAS PATRIAS

Location: Nationwide

Fiestas Patrias is Chile’s grand national holiday celebrated every September to commemorate independence. Cities come alive with parades, cueca dancing, and "fondas," where families share empanadas and traditional music. It embodies Chilean pride, unity, and heritage.
