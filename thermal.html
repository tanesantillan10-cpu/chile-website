Andes of Ñuble

Termas de Chillán

Termas de Chillán is one of Chile’s most famous thermal and ski resorts, located in the Andes of the Ñuble Region, about 82 km from the city of Chillán. It combines natural hot springs, wellness facilities, and outdoor adventure activities like skiing, trekking, and mountain biking, making it a year-round destination for relaxation and exploration.

Patagonia - Carretera Austral

Termas de Puyuhuapi

Termas de Puyuhuapi is a secluded hot springs lodge in Chilean Patagonia, located on Dorita Bay along the Carretera Austral. Accessible only by boat, it offers thermal waters, spa facilities, and excursions into Queulat National Park, making it a unique blend of wellness retreat and wilderness adventure.
